This project uses county-level childcare price data to communicate how childcare costs and affordability vary across the United States. The project includes a written analysis, infographic, blog-style report, and PowerPoint briefing designed for a non-technical policy audience.

**Tools Used:** Python, Plotly, Canva, PowerPoint, data visualization, written reporting

**Skills Shown:** Data storytelling, visual communication, audience-focused design, exploratory analysis, policy-focused reporting, infographic design, presentation design
